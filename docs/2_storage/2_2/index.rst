Central Storage
******************************

Central Storage is accessible via hardwired connections the Trigon Network, the High Performance Computing (HPC) Cluster, or a Full-Access EduVPN connection to Trigon. 
Central Storage is sometimes referred to as High Performance Storage. 
There are three Drives/Directories on the Central Storage which are relevant for Research Data Management.
